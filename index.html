Subie the Schnauzer


Subie is a 4 year old Miniature Schnauzer. He is obsessed with pinecones and fetch, and loves to sing along with sirens. Subie is a special Schnauzer becuase he has natural ears and a natural tail. Traditionally, Schnauzers have clipped ears and docked tails.
Many people who meet Subie do not believe that he is a Schnauzer beacuse of this, but we promise that he is!
